> About Me.
Hi! My name is Prerna Awasthi and I'm from New York City, but I was born in
Singapore and have also lived in Hong Kong and Salt Lake City.

majoring in Computer Science + Linguistics, and potentially minoring in Informatics.

I've been programming on and off since I was a little kindergartener trying out
Logo; I got into web development late in middle school and participated in Girls
Who Code in high school, which was ultimately what made me decide to major in CS,
and here I am!
